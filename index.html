About Me

Hi, I'm Bec. I'm a freelance content creator and trainee web developer from Melbourne, Australia. I'm an eCommerce specialist with 5 years experience in online stores, website building, user experience, workflows, and wireframing. I also design and produce content for both print and digital campaigns, and I've worn just about every marketing hat you can think of. I thrive on deadlines and work well autonomously and as part of a team. In my spare time I enjoy photography, travel, the oxford comma, and a good beach read.
